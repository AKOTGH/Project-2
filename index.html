Welcome

This website serves as a multipage project demo for LIS 500 coursework. It highlights foundational web development skills while engaging with issues of implicit bias, intersectionality, and equity in technology.

The site is designed for an academic audience, particularly first-year college students encountering these concepts for the first time.

Use the navigation above to explore our team, educational resources, and profiles of technology leaders advancing social justice.
